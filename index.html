Why Hubie Ballard

The Interdisciplinary Choice for UK's Future

As your current Board of Trustees representative, I bring a unique perspective that bridges clinical medicine, engineering innovation, and deep commitment to faculty success across all colleges.

With dual appointments in the College of Medicine and College of Engineering, I understand the challenges and opportunities facing faculty across disciplines. From the operating room to the engineering lab, I've built my career on interdisciplinary collaboration—the same approach I bring to university governance.

I've served in faculty leadership, managed high-stakes clinical programs, and championed innovation. Now I'm asking for your continued support to represent faculty interests at the highest level of university decision-making.
